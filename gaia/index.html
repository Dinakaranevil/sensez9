Sensez9 is founded on the belief that complex healthcare workflows can be better orchestrated with simple technology tools. Sensez9 is a mission driven company, providing Tech for Good, solutions that impact lives.

Sensez9 technology provides the mechanisms to manage, monitor, and optimize healthcare workflows in real time.

 

Improve Healthcare Delivery with Real Time Data & Digital Automation

Sensez9 helps healthcare providers manage patient access and flow, optimize operations and service delivery, and deliver better patient experiences.

Sensez9 solutions blend the power of Cloud SaaS platforms, Mobile applications, Connected Digital Sensors, Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, and Big Data Analytics. We help healthcare providers automate workflows, optimize frontline and support workforce, interconnect processes, and optimize productivity. We help healthcare providers improve patient engagement using technology.

Our Technology in Action



Care Access

Public Service Delivery App for SWPA 2-1-1

SWPA 2-1-1 is using a Sensez9 app to provide a streamlined interface of resources related to basic needs and the option to apply for COVID-19 relief funds or schedule Free Tax Prep appointments.

Care Coordination

Digital Health Operations for Large Hospital in Colombia

This 500-bed hospital is using Sensez9 software and mobile app to manage daily tasks of support teams and to orchestrate dynamic task requests across multiple departments, wings, floors, and patient rooms.
